Make health a priority

The WELL Building Standard is the leading tool for advancing health & well-being in buildings and communities globally.

Put people first

Since we spend 90% of our time indoors, the buildings where we live, work, learn and relax have a profound effect on our well-being: how we feel, what we eat and even how we sleep at night.

Create better buildings

WELL helps you take a human health-focused approach to the design, construction and operations of your building - with results. In a study of a WELL Certified office building, 92% of employees said that it had a positive effect on their health and well-being.

Lead the change

Wellness is the next trillion dollar industry, and WELL Certification can you help you or your organization take a leadership position in a growing movement.
